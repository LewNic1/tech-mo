About TechMo





Techmo was started by Nicolette Lewis a Licensed Clinical Social Worker, turned at-home mom and now career changer. Trying to balance being a student in a web development immersive bootcamp and motherhood, Nicolette found out the struggle is real, REAL quick! The struggle invoked a passion for Nicolette to create a platform where moms in tech bootcamps and seasonsed moms working in Tech can join forces to create community in sharing resources, creating study groups, network or enjoy a happy hour. Join TechMo today and become a mom who Tech(s)More!
